GardenMind is a mental wellness-centric application that hopes to reach out to people who are facing mental health issues or even any ordinary people. Anyone can relax in our application when they are experiencing negative emotions, and pen down their thoughts in a safe space.

Local Productive (iDP Project)

Our application Local Productive aims to provide local produce farming as a service, by giving users the opportunity to own their own garden (in a virtual form) and experience growing their local produce through leveraging gamification and using an incentivisation model. Through designing, building and managing, it is a sustainable circular agriculture solution that helps to boost the visibility of local farms and popularise local produce.
